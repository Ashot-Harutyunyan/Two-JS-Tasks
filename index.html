Task Elevator

There are three elevators and 20 floors in the building, people on each floor can order an elevator.

When ordering an elevator, move the elevator as close as possible to the floor where the elevator was ordered.

Example rules:

1. If the yellow person on the 6th floor orders an elevator,

elevator 2 will move to the 6th floor.

2. If the person on the 3rd floor orders an elevator, there are two

options, one of the elevators on the 1st and 3rd floors can move

• You can click on each floor or person, and the elevators will move accordingly.

• The elevators must move to the desired floor, people do not move.
